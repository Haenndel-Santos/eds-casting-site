# EDS Content Positioning Notes

Internal content guidance. Do not render publicly.

## Core Message

EDS helps industrial companies reduce technical and supplier risk in the sourcing of cast and forged components through engineering review, supplier coordination, quality follow-up and reliable supply communication.

## What EDS Brings

- Technical review before sourcing decisions
- Process knowledge for casting, forging, machining and finishing
- Redesign support for welded or assembled components
- Supplier coordination
- Production route clarification
- Documentation follow-up
- Supply continuity support

## How EDS Brings Results

- By identifying when a welded construction can be redesigned into casting
- By reducing machining and welding where technically possible
- By supporting material and process selection
- By coordinating with selected manufacturing partners
- By keeping drawings, tolerances and documentation requirements visible
- By supporting production follow-up before delivery
- By helping customers avoid supplier decisions based only on unit price

## Approved Wording

- EDS supported the redesign.
- EDS helped evaluate the sourcing route.
- EDS coordinated supplier communication.
- EDS supported quality documentation follow-up.
- EDS helped connect technical requirements with selected manufacturing partners.
- EDS supports engineering-driven sourcing for cast and forged components.

## Avoid Wording

- EDS manufactures parts in-house.
- EDS owns foundries.
- EDS guarantees cost reduction.
- EDS guarantees weight reduction.
- EDS guarantees lead time.
- EDS eliminates all risk.
- EDS is the cheapest supplier.
- We ignite.
- We team up.
- We get it.
- We got your back.
- We are resourceful.
- Any external supplier, partner or customer names without explicit approval.

## Technical Credibility Without Overclaiming

EDS should not present itself as a foundry or in-house manufacturer. The strongest positioning is that EDS brings casting and forging process knowledge into sourcing, redesign, supplier coordination and quality follow-up. Public copy should explain how EDS helps customers make better sourcing and redesign decisions rather than using vague brand slogans.

Approved wording:

- EDS reviews casting-oriented design factors such as parting line, draft, wall thickness, cores, machining allowance and tooling feasibility.
- EDS helps evaluate whether a welded or assembled construction could be redesigned into a cast or forged component.
- EDS coordinates technical discussions with selected manufacturing partners.
- EDS helps keep drawing requirements, material expectations and documentation needs visible during the sourcing route.
- EDS supports customers from technical review to supplier coordination and recurring supply communication.

Wording to avoid:

- EDS manufactures castings in-house.
- EDS owns a modelmakerij, foundry or forging facility.
- EDS guarantees savings.
- EDS eliminates all production risk.
- EDS is always the cheapest option.
- We ignite.
- We team up.
- We get it.
- Generic slogans without technical explanation.
