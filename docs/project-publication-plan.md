# EDS Project Publication Plan

Internal planning document. Do not render publicly.

## Recommended First Public Cases

### 1. Movable Bracket

Reason: Strong welded-to-cast redesign story with weight reduction, cost reduction, machining reduction and deadline relevance.

Why it is commercially strong: The case clearly explains an original welded construction problem and connects the redesign to measurable weight, cost and project-window outcomes.

Before publication, approve:

- Technical accuracy of the redesign description
- Confidentiality and permission to publish
- Result wording and all percentages
- Whether the 10-week project window can be stated publicly
- Approved image path and visual material permission
- Component name and industry wording

Image needed: Approved before/after or finished component image showing the movable bracket without confidential machine details.

Percentages: Confirm whether 54% weight reduction and 40% cost reduction may be published.

Industry: Agricultural machinery can be mentioned only after approval.

Component name: Movable bracket can be mentioned only after approval.

Risk level: Medium.

### 2. Clamp Housing

Reason: Strong technical story around fatigue, stress optimization and demanding heavy-industry application.

Why it is commercially strong: The case demonstrates engineering support beyond price comparison by focusing on vibration loading, fatigue-related risk, stress optimization and field usability.

Before publication, approve:

- Technical accuracy of fatigue and stress wording
- Permission to publish heavy-industry application context
- Result wording and all percentages
- Approved image path and visual material permission
- Whether replaceable sleeve and guide tip details are non-confidential

Image needed: Approved clamp housing image or detail image that does not expose confidential customer or supplier information.

Percentages: Confirm whether 17% weight reduction and 37% stress optimization may be published.

Industry: Foundation equipment / heavy industry can be mentioned only after approval.

Component name: Clamp housing can be mentioned only after approval.

Risk level: Medium.

### 3. Bearing Houses

Reason: Clear assembly simplification and casting concept story using one pattern and two core configurations.

Why it is commercially strong: The case connects a practical assembly problem to a controlled casting concept, showing how one pattern and different cores can support configuration flexibility.

Before publication, approve:

- Technical accuracy of one-pattern and core-configuration wording
- Permission to publish printing machinery context
- Result wording and all percentages
- Approved image path and visual material permission
- Whether the component name can be used publicly

Image needed: Approved bearing house image or neutral component image that shows the casting concept without customer-identifying context.

Percentages: Confirm whether 44% weight reduction and 52% cost reduction may be published.

Industry: Printing machinery can be mentioned only after approval.

Component name: Bearing houses can be mentioned only after approval.

Risk level: Medium.

Do not publish all cases at once. Start with three strong approved examples and expand only after visual and commercial review.

Each case should remain draft until EDS confirms technical accuracy, confidentiality, commercial permission, visual material permission, approved image path, metric approval, and public wording.
